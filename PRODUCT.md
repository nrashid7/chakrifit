# Product

## Register

product

## Users

ChakriFit serves Bangladesh government job seekers who need to quickly understand which public circulars are worth applying to. They may be anxious about eligibility, deadlines, age limits, education requirements, and whether a PDF circular contains details that affect them.

## Product Purpose

ChakriFit turns a resume into a private eligibility profile, scores official Teletalk government job circulars against that profile, and explains the fit in plain language. Success means candidates spend less time reading every circular and more time applying to roles they can reasonably pursue.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, and reassuring. ChakriFit should feel like a careful eligibility desk, not a loud job board or generic AI tool.

## Anti-references

Avoid playful career-app visuals, dark AI dashboards, purple gradient SaaS tropes, heavy glass effects, stock-photo job-search imagery, and official-government impersonation. The app must remain independent, credible, and task-focused.

## Design Principles

- Lead with confidence: scores, status, and verification cues should be visible before decorative content.
- Preserve trust: always keep official circular access and privacy language close to sensitive actions.
- Make eligibility scannable: group requirements by decision value, not by database shape.
- Reduce anxiety: loading, empty, and failed parsing states should explain what is happening and what to do next.
- Stay fast and familiar: product UI should use standard controls, crisp feedback, and restrained motion.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-visible focus, reduced-motion support, and mobile-first responsive layouts. English and Bangla copy must remain layout-safe with longer labels and variable text lengths.
